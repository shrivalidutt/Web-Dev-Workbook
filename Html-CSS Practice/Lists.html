Let's make pancakes!!

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a greased pan, pour batter, and cook until bubbles appear; flip and cook the other side.
  3. Serve with syrup or toppings of your choice
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
